Buttercream Icing

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups confectioner's sugar, sifted
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Optional: food coloring

Directions

  1. Cream together the butter until smooth
  2. Add confectioner's sugar until fully incorporated
  3. Beat in vanilla extract
  4. Pour in milk
  5. Beat for another 3-4 minutes
  6. Optional: add food coloring

Cream Cheese Icing

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of cream cheese, softened
  • 4 cups of sifted confectioner's sugar
  • 2 sticks of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Using an electric mixer, mix together cream cheese, sugar, and butter on low speed
  2. Mix on high speed until light and fluffy
  3. Reduce speed of mixer to low and add vanilla extract
  4. Mix on high speed until light and fluffy
  5. Store in fridge until stiff

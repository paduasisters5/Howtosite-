How to Bake a Cake

Baking a cake is easier than it looks! Follow these steps to make the best cake you've ever tasted.

Vanilla Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 stick of unsalted butter: softened
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Butter two 8 inch round cake pans
  3. Mix together flour, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl
  4. Beat together butter and granulated sugar with a hand mixer on medium speed until combined (1-2 minutes)

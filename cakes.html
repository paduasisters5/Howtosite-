Vanilla Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 stick of unsalted butter: softened
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Butter two 8 inch round cake pans
  3. Mix together flour, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl
  4. Beat together butter and granulated sugar with a hand mixer on medium speed until combined (1-2 minutes)
  5. Add eggs to butter and sugar and continue to beat together
  6. Reduce speed on hand mixer to low and add flour/baking powder/salt mixture
  7. Add milk and vanilla and beat until combined
  8. Separate batter into halves and pour into the two pans
  9. Bake for about 35 minutes or until golden brown


Red Velvet Cake

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil for the pans
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk (room temperature)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 1 teaspoon white distilled vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Butter or flour pan
  3. In one bowl, mix together flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder (dry ingredients)
  4. In another bowl, mix together oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vinegar, and vanilla (wet ingredients)
  5. Mix together wet and dry ingredients until batter is smooth
  6. Divide cake batter evenly among pans and place them into the oven
  7. Bake for about 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean
